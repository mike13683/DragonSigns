Background:

Sytner Mini Cardiff is part of the Sytner Group, providing new and used Minis in and around Cardiff and as part of a wider network.

Sytner Mini Cardiff engage Dragon Signs in two areas of their operation:

  1. Car graphics - The Mini is known for its quirky and unique car graphics which include the iconic mini stripes. When purchasing a mini, customers may require graphics to differentiate their mini to another of the same colour and spec.
  2. Promotions – whether we are adding promotional graphics to a vehicle or event details to a showroom window, we provide signage to assist with sale initiatives.

Outcome:

We’ve kitted out 1000’s of Minis with vehicle graphics over the years. An additional stripe or unique graphic to a mini can be a make or break deal to complete a car sale for Mini Sytner Cardiff; some customers want to drive away with additional graphics and Sytner Mini Cardiff need to be able to deliver the request in a short period of time.

Our promotional work provides a print and fit service which produces engaging and eye catching signage across the showroom and across some vehicles. We’ve supported summer campaigns through to January sale promotions using colourful, branded window signage and utilising existing showroom vehicles.
