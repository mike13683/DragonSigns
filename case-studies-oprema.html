Background:

Oprema is one of the largest UK distributors of CCTV, access control, intrusion, fire and networking products.

We have worked with Oprema for approximately 8 years and had the pleasure of watching their iconic brand, utilising bold colours and strong messaging, unfold as their business has grown from strength to strength.

Our relationship has seen us provide numerous vehicle graphics to a growing fleet and branding two office premises with eye catching designs and motivational artwork.

Outcome:

In 2017 Oprema moved premises and we were asked to provide a variety of signage to transform the unit into an identifiable office space for the brand. The project required a variety of signage materials (see product list below) that were used to bring the building to life utilising the brand and key messages throughout. In addition, we carefully deconstructed existing signage from the old premises, transported and reconstructed at the new premises.

We continue to work closely with Oprema, providing vehicles wraps on new vans as they enter the Oprema fleet and signage updates to their office “achievement wall”.
