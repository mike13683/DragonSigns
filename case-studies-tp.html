Background:

Travis Perkins (TP) is a recognisable British brand and with a history steeped in heritage it’s no wonder it has become the UK’s largest supplier of building materials.

Dragon Signs relationship with TP has also been built upon long-standing foundations. Established nearly a decade ago with a vehicle signage contract, the relationship has organically grown year on year and now, we provide Outside Signage and all Point of Sale materials to TP stores across the breadth and depth of the UK.

Outcome:

As the relationship continues to flourish, we currently service in excess of 650 TP stores; whether that be an opening of a new branch or a depot re-fit. The Point of Sale materials required per project can be in the 1000’s as they cover the stores product information, pricing and way-finding details. Delivering these high volumes of printing to deadlines requires careful planning and attention to detail, delivered by an experienced team.
