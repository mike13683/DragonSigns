Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Products and Signage

We are manufacturing and distributing PPE equipment and signage to assist key workers, business owners and organisations to safely fulfil their duties.

We are one of the first organisations in Wales to be producing Category 3, CE approved face visors that are NHS compliant. This essential piece of equipment provides a barrier against potential contamination from liquid droplets and particles. Lightweight, adjustable and comfortable, the visor is constructed from 0.5mm clear PETG, giving an optically clear vision. The soft foam band, with an adjustable velcro hook and loop system provides a one size fits all solution.

All our PPE products are designed to protect individuals and the public. We have a wide-range of office solutions, individual kits and driving options available that are built around your specific needs. These include:

  • Category 3 CE approved, NHS compliant, face visors
  • Desk dividers
  • Vehicle screen dividers
  • Sneeze screens
