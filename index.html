We are operating in-line with manufacturing guidelines set out by the Government and will continue to review these on a weekly basis.

Our employees that can, are working from home and our manufacturing and installation teams are adhering to social distancing measures when producing both PPE and signage.

You can still contact us using the usual means - by phone on 029 2022 6501 or email, using the email address sales@dragonsigns.com

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Products and Signage

Protect and communicate to help reduce the spread of Covid-19 with safety signage, desk divides, sneeze screens and NHS compliant face visors.

We are manufacturing a number of PPE products and safety signage at our Dragon Signs headquarters in Cardiff, with the aim of helping organisations all over the UK to protect their employees and the public from the spread of Covid-19.

We are one of the first organisations in Wales to be producing Category 3, CE approved face visors that are NHS compliant. This essential piece of equipment provides a barrier against potential contamination from liquid droplets and particles.

Our wide-range of office solutions, individual kits and driving options are built around your specific needs, please view our comprehensive brochure details for the full PPE product list and signage examples.
