Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Products and Signage

Protect and communicate to help reduce the spread of Covid-19 with safety signage, desk divides, sneeze screens and NHS compliant face visors.

We are manufacturing a number of PPE products and safety signage at our Dragon Signs headquarters in Cardiff, with the aim of helping organisations all over the UK to protect their employees and the public from the spread of Covid-19.

We are one of the first organisations in Wales to be producing Category 3, CE approved face visors that are NHS compliant. This essential piece of equipment provides a barrier against potential contamination from liquid droplets and particles.

Our wide-range of office solutions, individual kits and driving options are built around your specific needs, please view our comprehensive brochure details for the full PPE product list and signage examples. We also supply a selection of facemasks, disposable gloves and gowns, plus hand sanitiser.

Face Visors

An essential piece of protective equipment, the Face Visor provides a barrier against potential contamination from liquid droplets and particles.

This lightweight, adjustable and comfortable visor is constructed from 0.5mm clear PETG, giving an optically clear vision.

The soft foam band and adjustable velcro hook and loop system provides a one size fits all product.

Vechicle Screen Divides

The 5mm thick, clear polycarbonate screen divide is fixed between the driver and passenger, reducing the risk of spreading harmful substances, liquid droplets and particles between two people travelling in the same vehicle.
